Introduction to Scuttlebutt

A Love Story

Scuttlebutt is:
  • a secure, peer-to-peer protocol for sharing messages.
  • the common name for a new decentralized social network
  • old sea-slang for gossip
  • much, much more

Scuttlebutt is designed around humans and the natural ways communities function.


It is not designed for the whims of business or the limits of a central database
